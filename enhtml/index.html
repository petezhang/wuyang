Company profile:

WUYANG METALS CO., LTD is a large enterprises focus on stainless steel manufacturing, processing, foreign trade, technical services . The plants located in Jiangyin city of Jiangsu Province, which enjoy the most rich manufacturing resources and known as “first capital-strength country of China".

Our Technicians and leader of QC is from former Baosteel professionals .With year's of efforts in the field of production and lean production management, We have steady created a stable team full of experience of stainlesssteel production, processing and inspection. The company's stainless steel seamless pipes is able to meet the strict technical requirements of the customer, our product has gradually become recognized by the industry and are approved by users and industry experts at home and abroad.

Meanwhile, our sales network has grown from China mainland into the world market .Taiwan, South East Asia, Europe, the United States, South Korea, Australia, Malaysia, Brazil are the main countries WUYANG Metal served for projects with overseas partners.
