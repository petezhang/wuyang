Manufacturing capacity

Our Plants covers an area of 40000 square meters with two different workshops specialized in the manufacture of seamless stainless steel tube of small diameter and stainless seamless pipe of large diameter under bright annealed,polished and solution-annealed condition. The annual production capacity of WUYANG Metal is around 10000 tons, our annual yearly turnover is RMB 120millon. the production diameter range of the outer diameter is from 3mm-800mm, and wall thickness is from 0.5mm ~ 70mm.

Our plants are well equipped with advanced Machines,production technology and strong technical forces.  We strictly control the incoming raw materials and keep tight relationship of Baoshan Iron and Steel, Yongxing special steel, Outokumpu Ott, Sandvik, Posco, Sumitomo Metal, we always keeps closely attention from the raw material control throughout the life circle to ensure the stability and service life of the finished product. Our material has been approved by mechanical engineering companies for overseas project. Our partners and end users are also leading company worldwide known.

We can supply :

Austenitic stainless steel :304 / L, 304H, 304LN, 321 / 312H, 316 / 316L, 316H, 316Ti, 317 / L, 347 / 347H, 310 / 310S, 1.4401 / 1.4404, 1.4301 / 1.4307,1.4541,1.4571 ;

Duplex stainless steel: S31803, S32205, S32304, S31500, S32750, 1.4462;

Nickel-based alloy, Incoloy800H, Incoloy800, Incoloy600, 904L,

Product group are applicable to the shipbuilding industry, instrumentation, nuclear power, energy, oil petrochemical and chemical industry, ocean exploration, heat exchanger and boiler manufacturers.
